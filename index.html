    About

    Welcome to my Hijabi Lookbook!

    As a fellow hijabi, I understand the challenges we face in expressing our love for fashion while staying true to our values of modesty. It’s essential for us to find outfits that not only respect our beliefs but also celebrate our individuality and style.

    In this lookbook, you'll discover a curated collection of outfits designed for various occasions—from casual outings to festive celebrations and professional settings. Each look is crafted with care, ensuring that our skin remains covered while allowing us to express our personal style.

    My goal with this lookbook is to provide inspiration and ideas for hijabis seeking to dress beautifully and appropriately for every event. I believe that fashion should empower us, and I hope this lookbook serves as a guide for you to create your unique looks.

    Style Tips

    Wrapped style

    Step by step guide

    1.Wear an undercap to keep the hijab secure and prevent it from slipping.

    2.Place the hijab on your head with both ends equal in length. Adjust to ensure it frames your face neatly.

    3.Bring the two sides together under your chin and secure them with a small pin or tuck them comfortably.

    4.Take one side of the hijab and wrap it across the opposite shoulder, bringing it back around over your head and around the side of your face.

    5.Use a pin to secure the hijab on one side, just above or near your ear. Make sure it’s snug, but not too tight.

    6.Adjust the front for a smooth, even drape around your face. Let the remaining fabric fall naturally over your shoulder for a soft, graceful look.

    Tutorial

    One-Shoulder Drape

    Step by step guide

    1.Start by placing a hijab cap on your head to keep your hair in place and provide a secure base.

    2.Lay your hijab on your head with both sides of the scarf equal in length.

    3. Pin the two sides together under your chin for a snug and comfortable hold.

    4.Take one side of the hijab and gently place it over your shoulder, allowing the fabric to drape naturally.

    Tutorial

    Turkish Style

    Step by step guide

    1.Put on a snug undercap to keep your hair in place and provide a good base for the hijab.

    2.Take a square hijab and fold it diagonally into a triangle.

    3.Place the folded hijab over your head, making sure both sides are the same length in front.

    4.Secure the hijab under your chin with a small pin or safety pin.

    5.Take the two sides of the hijab, pull them to the back, and tie them comfortably at the nape of your neck.
